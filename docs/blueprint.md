# **App Name**: RevendaPro

## Core Features:

- Secure Administrator Authentication: Enable administrator login/logout with email and password using Firebase Authentication.
- Intuitive Dashboard Overview: Present a comprehensive summary of sales, finances (received, pending, overdue), customer count, and product performance.
- Client & Sales History Management: Add, edit, view, and delete client profiles, access their purchase history, and integrate quick WhatsApp contact.
- Product Catalog & Inventory Management: Maintain a detailed product catalog with names, brands, categories, pricing, photos, and descriptions.
- Efficient Sales & Order Processing: Facilitate recording new sales with client and product selection, various payment methods (including 'fiado' with due dates), discounts, and automatic total calculations.
- Financial Oversight & Payment Tracking: Provide a clear view of overall finances, tracking received, pending, and overdue payments, with features to update payment statuses.
- AI-Powered Customer Engagement Tool: Generate personalized WhatsApp marketing message drafts for individual clients, leveraging their purchasing behavior and product interests using an AI tool.

## Style Guidelines:

- Primary color: Dark elegant pink (#C2185B) for key actions and branding elements, conveying professionalism and femininity.
- Background color: A very soft, desaturated pink-beige (#F6F0F2) providing a clean, bright, and organized canvas that subtly hints at the primary hue.
- Accent color: A vibrant fuchsia-purple (#C046C6) to draw attention to important alerts, interactive elements, and highlight key information, harmonious with the primary.
- Neutral Text: Deep charcoal gray (#333333) for primary text content, ensuring optimal readability and a sleek look.
- UI Elements: Clean white (#FFFFFF) for cards and modals to create clear visual separation and reinforce modernity.
- Body and headline font: 'Inter' (sans-serif) for its modern, objective, and highly legible appearance across all data and textual content.
- Elegant, minimalistic line-art icons that complement the professional and clean aesthetic, prioritizing clarity and quick recognition of actions.
- Responsive, card-based layout inspired by modern management dashboards, featuring clear data visualization, organized sections, and slightly rounded interactive elements for a contemporary feel.
- Subtle, smooth transitions and micro-interactions for navigation and state changes, enhancing the user experience without being distracting.
